Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum eligibility for ITI courses?

The minimum eligibility for most ITI courses like Fitter, Electrician, and Welder is passing the 10th standard board examination with Mathematics and Science. Some courses may allow 8th pass candidates.

Are there any scholarships available?

Yes, we offer scholarships based on merit and financial need. State government scholarships for SC/ST/OBC and minority students are also applicable as per government norms.

Do you provide hostel facilities?

Yes, we have separate hostel facilities for boys and girls with 24/7 security, WiFi, and mess facilities. Limited seats are available on a first-come, first-served basis.

What about campus placements?

We offer 100% placement assistance. Our dedicated placement cell organizes campus drives with top manufacturing and tech companies like Tata Motors, Maruti Suzuki, L&T, and Siemens.
